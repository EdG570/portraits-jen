About me

I am a mother and a wife, a photographer and a friend. All of these things are my passion in life. My style is traditional and classic. When you hire me for one of the most important days in your life, I will be there for you every step of the way. I never book more than one wedding in a day, YOUR day is my priority.


I have over 10 years of experience with my Canon. I have been published, won awards and even launched my daughters modeling career with my images. Photography is my art and I take it very seriously. Let's meet, I am so looking forward to it.
